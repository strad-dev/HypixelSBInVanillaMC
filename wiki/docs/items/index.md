# Items

SkyBlock in Vanilla adds a wide variety of custom items across several categories. All items are obtained through crafting or boss drops.

## Categories

### [Weapons](weapons/index.md)
Three powerful weapons — the Hyperion, Dark Claymore, and Terminator — each with unique stats and enchantment scaling.

### [Armor](armor/index.md)
Seven mythic armor pieces crafted from boss drops. Mix and match pieces from Wither Lords, dragons, and slayer bosses.

### [Tools](tools/index.md)
Nine utility items including teleportation wands, healing staves, crowd control, and the Divine-tier Divan's Pickaxe.

### [Ingredients](ingredients/index.md)
Crafting materials dropped by bosses and mined from ores. Required for crafting weapons, armor, and tools.

### [Summon Items](summon-items/index.md)
Items used to summon bosses by renaming the correct mob type. Hard mode variants spawn when you have Bad Omen.
