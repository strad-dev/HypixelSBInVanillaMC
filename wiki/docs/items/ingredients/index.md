# Ingredients

Ingredients are crafting materials used to create the powerful items in SkyBlock in Vanilla. They are divided into three categories based on how they are obtained.

## Categories

<div class="stat-line"><strong>Mining Materials</strong> -- Rare drops from mining ores, used primarily in <a href="../tools/divan-pickaxe/">Divan's Pickaxe</a>. <a href="mining/">View all &rarr;</a></div>
<div class="stat-line"><strong>Misc Materials</strong> -- Drops from bosses and mobs, used across many recipes. <a href="misc/">View all &rarr;</a></div>
