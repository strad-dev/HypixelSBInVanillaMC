# Hard Mode

Hard Mode is a difficulty modifier that causes all summoned bosses to spawn their significantly harder variants with better [drop rates](custom-drops.md).

## Activation

Hard Mode is triggered when the player who summons a boss has the **BAD_OMEN** potion effect active. To get Bad Omen, kill a raid captain (an illager carrying a banner) without triggering a village raid.

## Effects

- The normal boss variant is replaced with its Hard Mode counterpart.
- Hard Mode bosses have significantly more HP.
- Hard Mode bosses gain new, unique abilities not present in their normal versions.
- [Drop rates](custom-drops.md) for rare items are substantially increased (typically 25-50% vs 2-10%).
