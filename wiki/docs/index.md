# SkyBlock in Vanilla

**Version 1.3.0** | Paper/Spigot 1.21.11 | Java 21

SkyBlock in Vanilla is a Minecraft plugin that brings Hypixel SkyBlock items, bosses, and mechanics to vanilla survival. No modded client required -- just drop it into your server and play.

## Key Features

- **Custom Weapons with Abilities** -- Wield iconic weapons like Hyperion, Terminator, and Dark Claymore, each with unique right-click and left-click abilities powered by the mana system.
- **8+ Ender Dragon Variants** -- Fight Protector, Superior, Young, Wise, and more, each with distinct mechanics and exclusive loot tables.
- **Wither Lords Boss Fight** -- Take on Maxor, Goldor, Storm, and Necron in a multi-phase encounter inspired by the Catacombs Floor 7 boss fight.
- **Slayer Bosses** -- Challenge powerful slayer-type bosses for rare crafting materials and gear upgrades.
- **Custom Mining System** -- Mine unique ores and materials used in SkyBlock crafting recipes.
- **Mana / Intelligence System** -- A full mana system with passive regeneration, ability costs, and action bar display, adding a resource management layer to combat.
