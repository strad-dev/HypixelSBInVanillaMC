# Voidcrazed Seraph

<div class="stat-line">HP: 1666 | Damage: 55 | Speed: 0.5</div>

*Hard Mode variant of: [Voidgloom Seraph](../generic/voidgloom-seraph.md)*

## Entity Type

Enderman

## Summoning

Use [Superior Remnant](../../items/summon-items/index.md) on an Enderman while having **BAD_OMEN**.

## Abilities

- **Enhanced Dissonance** -- deals **30 magic damage per second** to all players within 16 blocks (double the normal version's 15).
- **Flame Beam Phase (50% HP)** -- becomes invulnerable, stops moving, and spawns 4 rotating flame beams (16 blocks long) for 12 seconds. Each beam deals 4 absolute damage on contact.
- **Yang Glyph** -- every 30 seconds, drops a Beacon block nearby. You must **mine it within 10 seconds** or a 1000-damage TNT explosion detonates.
