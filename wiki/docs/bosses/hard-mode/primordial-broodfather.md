# Primordial Broodfather

<div class="stat-line">HP: 200 | Damage: 16 | Speed: 0.67</div>

*Hard Mode variant of: [Tarantula Broodfather](../generic/tarantula-broodfather.md)*

## Entity Type

Spider

## Summoning

Use [Spider Relic](../../items/summon-items/index.md) on a Spider while having **BAD_OMEN**.

## Abilities

- **Damage Reflection** -- reflects 1 absolute damage back on hit.
- **Harsher Damage Cap** -- incoming damage capped at 5 + (excess - 5) / 10 (stricter than the normal variant's /5).
- **Cocoon Phase (50% HP)** -- becomes invulnerable, creates a cobweb cage around the player, and deals 1-2 absolute damage every 0.5 seconds for 10 seconds.
- **On Death** -- spawns the [Conjoined Brood](conjoined-brood.md) (Phase 2).
