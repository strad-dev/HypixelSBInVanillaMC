# Atoned Horror

<div class="stat-line">HP: 200 | Speed: 0.5</div>

*Hard Mode variant of: [Revenant Horror](../generic/revenant-horror.md)*

## Entity Type

Zombie

## Summoning

Use [Atoned Flesh](../../items/summon-items/index.md) on a Zombie while having **BAD_OMEN**.

## Abilities

- **Regeneration** -- heals +1 HP every 0.35 seconds.
- **TNT Spam** -- spawns TNT on the nearest player every 3 seconds.
- **Nuclear Explosion** -- every 30 seconds, initiates a 7-second countdown. During the countdown, the boss is **invulnerable**. After the countdown, unleashes a **250-damage TNT burst**.
- **Damage Immunity** -- immune to ranged, magic, and ranged-special damage. **Only takes melee damage.**
