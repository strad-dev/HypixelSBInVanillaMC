# Enraged Chickzilla

<div class="stat-line">HP: 1000 | Scale: 4x</div>

*Hard Mode variant of: [Chickzilla](../generic/chickzilla.md)*

## Entity Type

Chicken (massive-sized)

## Summoning

Use [Omega Egg](../../items/summon-items/index.md) on a Chicken while having **BAD_OMEN**.

## Abilities

- **Full Damage Reflection** -- reflects **100%** of all damage back to the attacker (double the normal Chickzilla's 50%).

With 100% damage reflection, you will take as much damage as you deal. Healing and sustain are essential.
