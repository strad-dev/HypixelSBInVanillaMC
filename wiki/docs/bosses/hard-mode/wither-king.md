# Wither King

<div class="stat-line">HP: 2000 | Scale: 2x</div>

*Hard Mode variant of: [Wither Lords](../wither-lords/index.md)*

## Entity Type

Wither (stationary, double-sized)

## Summoning

Summon a Wither while having **BAD_OMEN**. The Wither King is the final boss of the plugin.

## Abilities

The Wither King stays stationary and relies on its henchmen and explosive attacks.

### Henchmen

The Wither King spawns **5 Henchmen** sequentially. Each has **666 HP** and wields a **Sharpness 7** sword:

1. **Power Henchman**
2. **Fire Henchman**
3. **Ice Henchman**
4. **Soul Henchman**
5. **Martial Henchman**

The Wither King's HP is locked at **(henchmen alive x 400)** until enough henchmen are defeated. You must kill henchmen to unlock damage on the King.

### Wither Guards

Periodically spawns Wither Guards with **150 HP**, Sharpness 5 swords, and shields.

### Boom

Periodic massive TNT explosions dealing **200 base damage**, reduced by 20 for each living henchman.
