# Zealot Bruiser

<div class="stat-line">HP: 999 | Damage: 45 | Speed: 0.5</div>

Enderman

## Summoning

Use [Corrupt Pearl](../../items/summon-items/index.md) on an Enderman while having **BAD_OMEN**. Also spawned during Phase 5 of the [Primal Dragon](../ender-dragons/primal.md) fight.

## Abilities

- **Void Pull** -- 20% chance to teleport the attacker to itself and deal **30 damage** (increased from the normal Zealot's 12).
