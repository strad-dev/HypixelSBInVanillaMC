# Bosses

SkyBlock in Vanilla features a wide variety of custom bosses across four categories. Each boss has unique mechanics, stats, and drop tables.

## Boss Categories

### [Ender Dragons](ender-dragons/index.md)

Eight dragon variants that spawn randomly when fighting the Ender Dragon in The End, plus the Primal Dragon hard mode boss. Superior Dragon is the rarest at 4% spawn chance, while the other seven share the remaining 96% equally.

### [Generic Bosses](generic/index.md)

Summonable bosses that are spawned by using a specific summoning item on a specific mob. Includes Revenant Horror, Tarantula Broodfather, Chickzilla, Zealot, meloG norI, Sadan, Voidgloom Seraph, and the Highly Infuriated Wither Skeleton.

### [Wither Lords](wither-lords/index.md)

Four unique Wither variants -- Maxor, Storm, Goldor, and Necron -- that spawn randomly with equal 25% chance when you summon a Wither.

### [Hard Mode](hard-mode/index.md)

Significantly harder versions of existing bosses that spawn when the summoner has the BAD_OMEN potion effect. Features unique mechanics, more HP, and better drop rates.
