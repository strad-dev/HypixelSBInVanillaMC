# Storm

<div class="stat-line">Spawn Chance: 25%</div>

## Entity Type

Wither

## Summoning

Spawns randomly (25% chance) when summoning a Wither.

## Abilities

- **Thunderstorm** -- sets weather to thunder when it spawns.
- **Periodic Lightning** -- spawns lightning periodically within a 64-block radius.
- **Lightning on Hit** -- 50% chance to summon a lightning bolt on the target when dealing damage.
- **Skull Lightning** -- wither skulls spawn lightning within a 32-block radius.

The weather-controlling Wither Lord. Extremely dangerous in open areas.

## Drops

Wither Lord materials.
