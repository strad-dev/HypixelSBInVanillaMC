# Maxor

<div class="stat-line">Movement Speed: 2.0 | Flying Speed: 2.0 | Spawn Chance: 25%</div>

## Entity Type

Wither

## Summoning

Spawns randomly (25% chance) when summoning a Wither.

## Abilities

- **Evasion (Melee)** -- 10% chance to teleport when hit by a melee attack.
- **Evasion (Ranged)** -- 5% chance to teleport when hit by a ranged attack.
- **Fast Skulls** -- wither skulls travel 3x faster than normal.

The fastest Wither Lord. Maxor is all about speed and evasion.

## Drops

Wither Lord materials.
