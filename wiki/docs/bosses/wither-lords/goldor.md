# Goldor

<div class="stat-line">Armor Toughness: 13 | Spawn Chance: 25%</div>

## Entity Type

Wither

## Summoning

Spawns randomly (25% chance) when summoning a Wither.

## Abilities

The most defensive Wither Lord. Goldor is a pure tank with no special attacks -- just extremely high durability from its Armor Toughness of 13. Expect a long fight.

## Drops

Wither Lord materials.
