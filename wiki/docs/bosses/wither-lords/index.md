# Wither Lords

The Wither Lords are 4 unique Wither variants that spawn randomly when you summon a Wither. Each has an equal **25% chance** to spawn. All Wither Lords ignore damage during invulnerability ticks.

| Wither Lord | Special | Description |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| [Maxor](maxor.md) | Speed 2.0 | The fastest Wither |
| [Storm](storm.md) | Lightning | The weather controller |
| [Goldor](goldor.md) | Armor Toughness 13 | The most defensive Wither |
| [Necron](necron.md) | +4 extra damage | The strongest Wither |

## Hard Mode

When summoning a Wither with BAD_OMEN, you will face the [Wither King](../hard-mode/wither-king.md) instead -- the final boss of the plugin.
