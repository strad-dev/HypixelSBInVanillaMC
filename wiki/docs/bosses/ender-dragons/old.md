# Old Dragon

<div class="stat-line">HP: 300 | Spawn Chance: ~13.7%</div>

## Entity Type

Ender Dragon

## Summoning

Spawns randomly when fighting the Ender Dragon in The End. One of the seven common dragons sharing 96% total spawn chance.

## Abilities

The Old Dragon has no special abilities. It is a pure tank dragon with the highest base HP of any non-hard-mode dragon.
