# Primal Dragon

<div class="stat-line">HP: 1000 | Mode: Hard Mode</div>

## Entity Type

Ender Dragon

## Summoning

Spawns when fighting the Ender Dragon in The End while having the **BAD_OMEN** potion effect. This is the hard mode variant of the dragon fight.

## Abilities

The Primal Dragon is a dialogue-driven, phased boss fight and one of the most challenging encounters in the plugin. It features full dialogue throughout the fight and progressively escalating mechanics.

### Phase 1 (1000-800 HP)

Basic attacks with **TNT** spawning on players.

### Phase 2 (800-600 HP)

TNT continues. **Fireball spam** begins targeting players.

### Phase 3 (600-400 HP)

**TNT rain** is added on top of the fireball spam. The arena becomes increasingly dangerous.

### Phase 4 (400-200 HP)

**Zealots** are summoned to fight alongside the dragon. TNT and fireballs continue.

### Phase 5 (200-0 HP) -- The Final Trick

The most dangerous phase:

- **End Crystals** spawn and heal the dragon by +10 HP each.
- **Extreme TNT bombardment** covers the arena.
- **Fireballs target all players** simultaneously.
- **[Zealot Bruisers](../hard-mode/zealot-bruiser.md)** join the fight (hard mode Zealots with 999 HP).

### "Unfair!" Damage Reduction

The Primal Dragon progressively becomes harder to damage as the fight goes on:

| Damage Type | Phase 1 | Phase 2 | Phase 3 | Phase 4 | Phase 5 |
|-------------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| Melee | 90% reduction | -- | -- | -- | 50% reduction |
| Ranged | 25% reduction | -- | -- | -- | 8% reduction |
| Magic | 90% reduction | -- | -- | -- | 25% reduction |

### Perch Damage Cap

When the Primal Dragon is perched, a maximum of **50 damage** will force it to leave the perch.
