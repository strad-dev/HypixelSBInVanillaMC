# Unstable Dragon

<div class="stat-line">HP: 200 | Spawn Chance: ~13.7%</div>

## Entity Type

Ender Dragon

## Summoning

Spawns randomly when fighting the Ender Dragon in The End. One of the seven common dragons sharing 96% total spawn chance.

## Abilities

- **Lightning on Summon** -- spawns lightning across a 128-block radius when it appears.
- **Lightning Fireballs** -- 50% chance to spawn lightning on fireball impact.

Extremely dangerous in open areas. The lightning strikes can catch you off guard.
