# Superior Dragon

<div class="stat-line">HP: 250 | Armor: 5 | Spawn Chance: 4%</div>

## Entity Type

Ender Dragon

## Summoning

Spawns randomly when fighting the Ender Dragon in The End. The **rarest dragon** with only a **4% spawn chance**.

## Abilities

- **Regen I** -- passively regenerates health over time.
- **Lightning Fireballs** -- fireballs have a 25% chance to spawn lightning on impact.
- **Extra Damage** -- deals 3 extra damage per hit.

The rarest and most dangerous dragon. Defeating it is a true achievement.

## Drops

| Drop | Chance | Link |
|------|--------|------|
| [Superior Remnant](../../items/summon-items/index.md) | 100% | Summoning item |
| Dragon Egg | 100% | Trophy |

The Superior Dragon is the only dragon that **always** drops a Superior Remnant. Other dragons only have a 2% chance.
