# Protector Dragon

<div class="stat-line">HP: 200 | Armor: 10 | Spawn Chance: ~13.7%</div>

## Entity Type

Ender Dragon

## Summoning

Spawns randomly when fighting the Ender Dragon in The End. One of the seven common dragons sharing 96% total spawn chance.

## Abilities

The Protector Dragon has no special attacks. Its high armor value (10) makes it very tanky, resulting in a longer fight that poses little direct threat.
