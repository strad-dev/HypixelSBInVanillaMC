# Holy Dragon

<div class="stat-line">HP: 200 | Spawn Chance: ~13.7%</div>

## Entity Type

Ender Dragon

## Summoning

Spawns randomly when fighting the Ender Dragon in The End. One of the seven common dragons sharing 96% total spawn chance.

## Abilities

- **Regen I** -- passively regenerates health over time.

The Holy Dragon has no special attacks. It is a passive dragon and the easiest dragon to fight.
