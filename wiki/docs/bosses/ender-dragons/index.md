# Ender Dragons

All ender dragons spawn randomly when you fight the Ender Dragon in The End. The **Superior Dragon** has a **4% spawn chance**; the other 7 dragons share the remaining **96% equally** (~13.7% each).

## Dragon Variants

| Dragon | HP | Special |
|--------|----|---------|
| [Holy Dragon](holy.md) | 200 | Regen I |
| [Old Dragon](old.md) | 300 | Tank |
| [Protector Dragon](protector.md) | 200 | Armor 10 |
| [Strong Dragon](strong.md) | 200 | +4 extra damage |
| [Superior Dragon](superior.md) | 250 | Rarest (4%), lightning, +3 damage |
| [Unstable Dragon](unstable.md) | 200 | Lightning on spawn and fireballs |
| [Wise Dragon](wise.md) | 200 | No special abilities |
| [Young Dragon](young.md) | 200 | 3x fireball speed |

## Hard Mode

| Dragon | HP | Special |
|--------|----|---------|
| [Primal Dragon](primal.md) | 1000 | Multi-phase fight |

The Primal Dragon is the hard mode variant, triggered by having BAD_OMEN. It is one of the most challenging encounters in the plugin.
