# Young Dragon

<div class="stat-line">HP: 200 | Spawn Chance: ~13.7%</div>

## Entity Type

Ender Dragon

## Summoning

Spawns randomly when fighting the Ender Dragon in The End. One of the seven common dragons sharing 96% total spawn chance.

## Abilities

- **Fast Fireballs** -- fireballs travel 3x faster than normal.
- **Evasion** -- 5% chance to teleport 16 blocks when damaged.

The speed-focused dragon. Its fast projectiles are difficult to dodge.
