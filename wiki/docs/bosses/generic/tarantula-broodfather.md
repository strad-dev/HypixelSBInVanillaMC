# Tarantula Broodfather

<div class="stat-line">HP: 100 | Damage: 10 | Speed: 0.5</div>

## Entity Type

Spider

## Summoning

Use [Spider Relic](../../items/summon-items/index.md) on a Spider.

## Abilities

- **Damage Reflection** -- reflects 1 damage back to the attacker on each hit.
- **Damage Cap** -- incoming damage above 5 is heavily reduced: 5 + (excess - 5) / 5. This means overkill damage is significantly weakened.
- **Teleport** -- teleports when damaged.
