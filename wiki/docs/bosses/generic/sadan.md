# Sadan

<div class="stat-line">HP: 400 | Damage: 24 | Speed: 0.5 | Scale: 6x | KB Resistance: 100%</div>

## Entity Type

Zombie (Giant) -- massive zombie wearing full diamond armor and wielding a diamond sword with Knockback 2. Has a boss bar.

## Summoning

Use [Giant Zombie Flesh](../../items/summon-items/index.md) on a Zombie.

## Abilities

- **Random Attacks** -- 25% chance on each hit to trigger one of four special attacks (25% chance each):
    - **Stomp** -- deals 25 damage.
    - **Laser Beam** -- deals 25 damage.
    - **Sword Attack** -- deals 25 damage.
    - **Anvil Rain** -- rains anvils on the player.
