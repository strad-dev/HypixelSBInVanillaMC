# meloG norI

<div class="stat-line">HP: 50 | Damage: 25</div>

## Entity Type

Iron Golem ("Iron Golem" spelled backwards)

## Summoning

Use [Antimatter](../../items/summon-items/index.md) on an Iron Golem.

## Abilities

- **Melee Only** -- only takes damage from melee attacks. All other damage types are ignored.
- **Overhealing** -- if you deal more than 5 damage, the excess heals the boss instead. Hit it gently!
- **Counter-Attack** -- 15% chance to deal 20 damage back to the attacker.
- **Teleport** -- 25% chance to teleport to the attacker.
