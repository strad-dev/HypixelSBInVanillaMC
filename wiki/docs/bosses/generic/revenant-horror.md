# Revenant Horror

<div class="stat-line">HP: 100 | Damage: 15 | Speed: 0.4</div>

## Entity Type

Zombie (wearing chainmail armor, wielding iron sword with Knockback 2)

## Summoning

Use [Atoned Flesh](../../items/summon-items/index.md) on a Zombie.

## Abilities

- **AOE Attack** -- deals 10 damage every second to all players within 8 blocks. Damage increases to **20** when below 50% HP.
